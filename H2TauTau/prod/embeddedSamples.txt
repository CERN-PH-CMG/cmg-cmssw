
**********Fix from simone to produce PFAODs in CMSSW_4_4_4:

The recipe should be the following:
cvs co -r V04-05-07 JetMETCorrections/Type1MET
cvs co -r V02-03-00 JetMETCorrections/Algorithms                    
rm JetMETCorrections/Algorithms/interface/L1JPTOffsetCorrector.h
rm JetMETCorrections/Algorithms/src/L1JPTOffsetCorrector.cc
cvs co -r V03-01-00 JetMETCorrections/Objects       
cvs co -r CMSSW_4_2_4_patch1 DataFormats/METReco
cvs up -r 1.28 DataFormats/METReco/src/classes.h
cvs up -r 1.25 DataFormats/METReco/src/classes_def.xml
+
You have to modify the file classes_def.xml
under DataFormats/METReco/src/
in this way:
diff -r1.25 classes_def.xml
137c137
<   <class name="reco::CSCHaloData" ClassVersion="10">
---
> <class name="reco::CSCHaloData" ClassVersion="11">
138a139
> <version ClassVersion="11" checksum="3624507518"/>

So basically the dictionary for CSCHaloData would be like:
<class name="reco::CSCHaloData" ClassVersion="11">
<version ClassVersion="10" checksum="3522217120"/>
<version ClassVersion="11" checksum="3624507518"/>
</class>
<class name="edm::Wrapper<reco::CSCHaloData>"/>
